Three Generations of Trust

A Legacy Carried Forward

Pannalal Omprakash & Sons is a heritage brand built on three generations of trust, craftsmanship, and customer loyalty. Founded by our grandfather, Pannalal Omprakash, the business was later carried forward by our father and uncle, and today continues to grow under the next generation.

Known for our timeless sarees and suits, we have evolved into a complete destination for women's fashion, offering an expanded collection of designer suits, ethnic wear, and modern Indo-Western styles. Every piece is carefully selected to reflect elegance, comfort, quality, and authenticity—values that have defined our brand for decades.
